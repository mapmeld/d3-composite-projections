Landscape

Based on images from Department of Statistics Malaysia, more "tip-to-tip" view. East Malaysia actually has more land area than Peninsular Malaysia. Both projections are downscaling it, but this one is 0.76 to the original's 0.615. There was a little discrepancy here which makes me think that DOSM's East Malaysia is using a non-Mercator projection.

Portrait

Based on images from Department of Statistics Malaysia, with an angular divider. East Malaysia is 0.8x scale of Peninsular Malaysia.
